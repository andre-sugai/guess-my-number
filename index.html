How to play Guess My Number 🕹

Enter a number between 1 and 20 in the box, then click Check!. For each mistake, your score decreases by 1 point, when you hit the number in the box with a ? your score will go to the highscore, when you play again you will only enter the highscore if you make a score above the previous one. So the faster you hit the more points you'll get. Good luck! 🍀
